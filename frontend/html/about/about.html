Our Story

How Tossel Began

Built by commuters, for commuters

Tossel was born from a simple frustration: navigating public transportation shouldn't be complicated. Our founders, daily commuters themselves, experienced firsthand the challenges of planning multi-modal journeys, dealing with unexpected delays, and comparing travel costs.

In 2025, they assembled a team of transportation experts, developers, and designers to create a solution. The result is Tossel - a platform that brings together all modes of transportation into one seamless experience.

Today, Tossel serves over 50,000 daily users across 15 cities, helping them save time, money, and reduce their environmental impact. But we're just getting started.
